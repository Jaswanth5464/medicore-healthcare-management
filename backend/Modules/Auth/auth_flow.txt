MEDI-CORE HMS: AUTHENTICATION MODULE FLOW
========================================

This is a simple explanation of how users log in and stay secure in the system.

1. REGISTRATION (Signing Up)
----------------------------
- You provide your Name, Email, and Phone.
- You choose a Role (like Patient or Doctor).
- The system takes your password and "hashes" it (turns it into a secret code).
- We never store your actual password—only the secret code.

2. LOGIN (Getting access)
-------------------------
- When you type your email and password, the system checks if they match the secret code in the database.
- If they match, the system gives you two "Digital Keys":
    a) ACCESS TOKEN: This is your ID card. It allows you to see data. It is very secure but only lasts 15 minutes.
    b) REFRESH TOKEN: This is a "Stay Logged In" ticket. It is saved in the database so the system remembers you.

3. ACCESSING DATA
-----------------
- Every time your browser asks for information (like "Show my appointments"), it automatically sends your "Access Token" to the server.
- The server checks the token. If it's valid, it sends the data. If not, it says "Access Denied."

4. LOGOUT
---------
- When you logout, the "Refresh Token" in the database is destroyed (Revoked).
- Your digital keys become useless, and you are safely signed out.

SECURITY BENEFITS
-----------------
- Safe Passwords: No one (not even the admin) can see your real password.
- Secure Keys: The digital tokens use high-level encryption (JWT).
- Role Security: Patients cannot access Admin data.
